The Machine Gallery

Mobile App

Les Machines de l'île, a totally new artistic project. Born from the imagination of François Delaroziere and Pierre Orefice, it is located at the crossroads of the "invented worlds" of Jules Verne, the mechanical universe of Leonardo da Vinci and the industrial history of Nantes, on the exceptional site of the former shipyards.

As part of Nantes' cultural heritage, anchored in the regional landscape, Les Machines de l'île wanted to enhance the visitor experience in the Galerie des Machines. The aim was to create an aid to the visit and a better understanding of the Galerie des Machines, especially for foreign visitors.
