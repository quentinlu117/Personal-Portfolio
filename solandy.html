Solandy West

iPad App

As professionals in the design and production of custom-made sporting goods, Solandy West wanted to develop a quick solution to digitize feet to create custom sports insoles directly from Solandy West's partner sports stores.

A mobile iOS application in BtoBtoC has been developed especially for their needs. Associated with a 3D scanner, it allows a fast solution for digitizing the foot. The challenge was to display and manipulate 3D scenes using Apple's framework provided for this purpose, and then to perform matrix calculations on the scanned objects in order to create custom soles. On the second hand, I worked on the UX to make the Solandy West mobile application easy to use for sales partners, who use this app directly in-store with their customers.
