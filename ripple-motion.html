Ripple Motion

Branding - Web

Ripple Motion was founded in 2008, and has had a similar identity for 10 years. It is important for a company to update its corporate image in order to show its customers that it is able to evolve and share modern values. So I was able to take over the complete graphic redesign of the agency with my colleague in charge of marketing: logo, communication supports, business cards, office decoration...

The biggest but most important work was the redesign of the agency's website. And this redesign was mainly beneficial for SEO:

- significant increase in google search results in one year on targeted queries.
- Better visibility on the Google search engine
- More telephone and e-mail enquiries for potential projects.
